📄 Published Conference Paper

Cost-Benefit Analysis of Cold Site vs. Hot Site Disaster Recovery Strategies: A Framework for CISO Decision Making

Authors: Rishav Bhattarai, [Co-author if applicable] | Conference: [Conference Name] | Year: 2025

📝 Abstract

This paper presents a comprehensive analytical framework for Chief Information Security Officers (CISOs) to evaluate and justify disaster recovery (DR) investments. We develop an interactive cost-benefit model comparing Cold Site (slower recovery, lower operational cost) and Hot Site (immediate recovery, higher operational cost) strategies.

Our methodology quantifies the total cost of ownership (TCO) including infrastructure costs, downtime costs based on Recovery Time Objectives (RTO) and Recovery Point Objectives (RPO), and opportunity costs. The framework enables executives to make data-driven DR investment decisions by translating technical recovery metrics into financial impact.

Key Contributions: (1) A mathematical model for TCO comparison of DR strategies, (2) An interactive calculator tool for real-time scenario analysis, (3) Evidence-based recommendations for CISO budget defense and board presentations.
