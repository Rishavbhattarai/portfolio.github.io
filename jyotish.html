Bhumika · Introduction

The three branches of Jyotish

Before Hora can be practiced, the text situates it among the three limbs of the science of light.

01

SiddhantaMathematical Astronomy

The pure celestial mechanics of Jyotish — tracking exact planetary positions, orbits, eclipses and the shifting of the earth's axis (Ayanamsha). Texts like the Surya Siddhanta belong here.

02

SamhitaMundane Astrology

Macro-level effects of the sky on nations and nature: wars, political shifts, economic cycles, astro-meteorology, omens, and Vastu Shastra.

03

HoraPredictive · Natal

Interpreting an individual birth chart (Jatak Shastra) and Muhurta, the art of choosing auspicious moments. This is the branch BPHS itself teaches.
