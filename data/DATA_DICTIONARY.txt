
# DATA DICTIONARY
=================

## 1. nepal_migration_data.csv
Columns:
- Year: Academic year (2010-2024)
- Destination: Country name (Australia, USA, Japan, South Korea, UK)
- Students: Number of Nepali students in that country

## 2. field_of_study.csv
Columns:
- Field: Academic field name
- Percentage: Percentage of students in this field
- Avg_Annual_Cost_USD: Average annual tuition cost in USD

## 3. nepal_indicators.csv
Columns:
- Year: Calendar year (2010-2024)
- GDP_Per_Capita_USD: Nepal's GDP per capita in USD
- Remittance_Billion_USD: Total remittances received (in billions)
- Unemployment_Rate: Unemployment rate as percentage

## 4. forecast_2025_2030.csv
Columns:
- Year: Future year (2025-2030)
- Predicted_Students: Predicted total students abroad

## 5. economic_impact_analysis.csv
Columns:
- Year: Calendar year
- Total_Students: Sum of students across all destinations
- YoY_Growth: Year-over-year growth rate (%)
- GDP_Per_Capita_USD: GDP per capita
- Remittance_Billion_USD: Total remittances
- Unemployment_Rate: Unemployment rate (%)
- Total_Education_Cost_Million_USD: Annual education expenditure
- Nepal_GDP_Billion_USD: Total GDP estimate
- Cost_as_Pct_GDP: Education cost as % of GDP
- Cost_as_Pct_Remittance: Education cost as % of remittances
- Permanent_Brain_Drain: Students not returning (70%)
- Cumulative_Brain_Drain: Total cumulative loss

## 6. destination_statistics.csv
Columns:
- Destination: Country name
- Students_2010: Students in 2010
- Students_2024: Students in 2024
- Total_Growth_Pct: Total percentage growth
- CAGR_Pct: Compound Annual Growth Rate
- Market_Share_2024_Pct: Market share in 2024

DATA NOTES:
- All monetary values are in USD
- Growth rates are percentages
- Brain drain rate assumption: 70% non-return
- Average education cost: $34,000/year for 3 years
